Expeditions

What we offer

A challenging outdoor adventure, built with a set of learning outcomes in mind. Expect engaging days performing a range of outdoor activities which put your people in the heart of beautiful natural scenery.

With plenty of time to do so, participants are encouraged to lead the group, as they become proficient in a range of technical skills, and work together to overcome difficulties on their way. The focus remains heavily on self-reliance, and staff work to facilitate the group as they organise themselves.

Ideal for young people of moderate or above experience aged 11-21.
 

The benefit

Shorter (1-10 day) programs, involving 10-25 participants per group. Choose from a wide range of beautiful locations and take part in a variety of disciplines, or cover the distance with just one. Journey's scale to as many groups as you desire.

    Journeys

    What we offer

    A short program, bursting with opportunities to learn and have fun. Enjoy long days spent in picturesque settings, working as a team to accomplish your day's goals, and pushing individuals out of their comfort zones.

    Our talented staff will help your group work towards big activities in incremental steps, and facilitate the whole group to support one another and achieve their goal. Teach skills which sequence to later programs, or their own recreational time.

    Ideal for young people of all abilities aged 7-21.
     

    The benefit

    Custom Programs

    What we offer

    Delivery of atypical programs which fit your organisation's aims perfectly. Begin from your own locations, activities, and educational objectives. Then let us help you create a program around them which meets our risk framework and activity standards, so you can rest easy when your young people leave for their adventure.

    Custom programs involve more planning from you, but with our dedicated staff, the experience remains stress free. On program, we use the same experienced facilitators and leaders as all our other programs.

    Ideal for young people of all abilities aged 7-21.
