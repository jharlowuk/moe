Service Learning


Focus on empowering people, learning from others, and tackling their own problems as a group.

What we offer

A trip that takes your young people and staff to a carefully chosen service project, which aligns to your organisations culture. Give them meaningful and impactful work to do, that requires hard work to complete. Under the supervision of our expert staff, participants are encouraged to lead the group, organise themselves, and plan together to overcome challenges. Engage long or short term with the project, and see the results of your peoples hard work year after year.

Ideal for young people of all abilities aged 14-21.
 

The benefit

In completing service, participants will work with a diverse team, often encountering significant physical, communication, and mental challenge in completing their project. In doing so, they become more resilient, compassionate, and driven people, with a heightened appreciation for others needs. The project itself can be chosen in line with your organisations needs. From a building project with clear progress to public awareness work, MOE can help deliver a program which both meets your educational needs and aligns with your organisational values.
