Traditional Outdoor Education

Sleep under the stars, and spend the day immersed in the natural world.

Whether it's hiking mountains or canoeing expeditions, we specialise in delivering a broad range of activities in a variety of locations. Our flagship style, Monomyth Outdoor Education programs have proven educational value, and significant participant impact.
 

Expeditions

Long format (10+ day) programs, involving 10-14 participants per group. Traverse a stunning venue over your time as the group takes part in a variety of disciplines. Every moment get's used as your young people learn to take responsibility for themselves, and lead their own group.
 

Journeys

Shorter (1-10 day) programs, involving 10-25 participants per group. Choose from a wide range of beautiful locations and take part in a variety of disciplines, or cover the distance with just one. Journey's scale to as many groups as you desire.
 

Custom Programs

Plan and deliver atypical programs of any length and size with the same industry leading risk management, activity standards, and educational delivery as our journeys and expeditions.

Service Learning

Make a meaningful difference, friendships, and see the results of your work year on year.

Station a 12-20 volunteers per group at a domestic or international service project for 7-30 days. Choose your style of accommodation, and involve yourself over the several years to see your project flourish. Include optional activities at the end of your trip.

Overlanding

The master of everything, incorporating adventure, culture, and service into one package.

Take to the road with a group of 10-15 participants per group, in a beautiful international destination, or domestically. For 14-21 days, include a variety of adventure, cultural, and service activities, and pick a style of catering and accommodation to suit your group.
