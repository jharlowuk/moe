What makes every Trails trip special

 


Two trippers & an awesome group

There's no two ways about it, having a group of like-minded adventurers including two experienced leaders is the best way to thru-hike! We think our combination of trippers and community is the best in the business, and we're certain you won't find this ratio of instruction anywhere else.
 


Confidence and peace of mind

Taking on a thru-hike can be a daunting thing! With Trails, you can arrive knowing you have a large group (and two trippers) to support you to the finish line. Plus, you avoid the hassle of planning your route, food, kit, campsites, and resupplys.
 


Tested support

Every trip receives support from a trip coordinator who can help the group quickly and effectively should anything go awry. We know how important staying with the group is to our participants, and we'll work our backsides off to ensure you finish with your group too.

Whatever you choose, we'll see you out here.

Explorer Trips

1-2 Weeks - 10 per group - 1 layover day

This introduction to wilderness tripping will offer you plenty of time to learn and practice a variety of new skills. Taking responsibility on your journey will mean getting to grips with navigation, campcraft skills, and backcountry cooking. But you won't be learning these skills alone, with lots of time for walking, you'll get to know your group intimately, making friends out of teammates.

See the trips

Pathfinder Trips

2-3 Weeks - 10 per group - 3 layover days

A next-level challenge those already familiar with basic campcraft skills. You will travel over 19 days through challenging terrain, handling whatever weather gets thrown your way. As such, Pathfinder trips are all about helping one another physically and emotionally to accomplish your overall goal. Through doing so, you'll emerge from your journey not only proud, but with strong bonds with your peers.

See the trips

Voyageur Trips

4 Weeks - 8 per group - 4 layover days

These are advanced trips designed for those who have completed a Pathfinder (or in some cases Explorer) trip. The remote route for these trips allows you to become more comfortable in wilderness and continue to practice your navigation and campcraft skills. You will take on a large leadership role on the trip with the guidance of experienced and knowledgeable staff. Needless to say, you will make some cherished memories and friendships on a Voyageur trip.

See the trips

Serendipity Trips

6 Weeks - 8 per group - 6 layover days

These leaders travel through remote areas that are accessible by walking, paddle, or plane only. They will experience vast wilderness in all its glory. These groups will learn advanced navigation and campcraft skills and be fully involved in every aspect of the planning, implementation and completion of the trip. This truly is the experience of a lifetime!

Serendipity trips are by invite only.
