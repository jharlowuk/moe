What to expect

 

The application process

We like to get to know you a little bit before we sign you on so we know you're the right fit for your trip. Anticipate a few emails and a phone call before we officially register you. Sometimes we might recommend another expedition to you based on that information, but obviously this is just a suggestion!
 

Your fees

We have a variety of payment plans and options to suit everyone. All fees are due prior to the trail start date. If you have any questions about payment before you sign on, we hope we can be of service.
 

Pre-trip planning

Once you're registered, we'll put you in touch with your trippers who will be able to advise you on how to prepare for your journey, answer your questions, and may chose to put you in touch with other participants. It's a great opportunity to get a head start on the trip!
