What we do

We run outdoor programs for schools, which prepare their young people for the personal, social, and environmental challenges in their lives.

 

Discovering themselves

Whether it's or scaling a mountain, or simply making a meal, we want our adventures to get young people comfortable with being outside of their comfort zone. We believe that appropriate challenge gives our young people an opportunity to grow those skills of persistence, resilience, and grit which only come from experience.
 

Living with others

Through living in a new place and among one another, our young people learn to accommodate and respect other peoples differences, and, in the process, become friends. While getting along with their peers, our leaders help every group member develop their follower-ship, leadership, and confidence.
 

Exploring the natural world

All our trips make true use of the best classroom in the world, the outdoors. Taking our groups to beautiful and challenging environments helps them to both connect with nature, and learn their inner nature.

Educational Delivery

Our programs are structured learning experiences with clear aims. Working with schools, we develop outlines that put your learning objectives front and centre.

 

A valuable, modern curriculum

Using our syllabus framework, or with your own, the first thing our clients do is choose their educational outcomes. More than technical skills, our focus is always on skills that make young people future ready. Through this, we aim to make your young people's outdoor education as indispensable as their traditional one.
 

A part of your school, not an addition

We act in partnership with schools, teachers, and parents, to make our programs an extension of every students classroom. Each program is tailored to a schoolâ€™s culture, development goals, and participants abilities to ensure that we have the maximum possible impact on their students lives.
 

Sequenced programs

For clients sending their young people year after year, we offer outlines which build onto the curriculum of each student's previous program. By sequencing outdoor learning, we can safely guide students through increasingly difficult outdoor challenges, which result in impressive physical achievements and carefully planned personal development goals.

Risk Management

Safety is our overriding responsibility, so although we push our participants outside of their comfort zones, we always keep them within arms reach.

 

An integrated, proven risk framework

Our approach to risk management involves every component of our organisation to deliver a framework which is simultaneously hyper venue aware, and deeply scalable to client needs. We use the best systems and standards from across the globe to ensure that every program remains within your risk tolerances.
 

Support that never sleeps

With required daily communication, we're connected to every group wherever they are and whatever they're doing 24 hours a day. In the field, our program support staff are on standby, and helping out our group leaders. They deliver invaluable local knowledge, logistical support and tried and tested assistance on the ground.
 

Industry leading standards

All of our program activities adhere to a set of operating standards which match or exceed industry expectations. We carefully select, inspect, and monitor our venues prior and during the trip. If something does go wrong, our people and procedures are a safe pair of hands.
 

Insurance you can count on

All of our program are carefully insured. If you need to end the trip, or an accident happens, your group will be covered.

Ethical Responsibility

Practicing what we preach means considering our environmental and social impact both administratively, and in the field.

 

Wilderness conservation

In the field, we are committed to minimising our environmental impact by following the seven "Leave No Trace" principles. By teaching and adhering to these standards, we aim to inspire a new generation of conservationists.
 

Social responsibility

We support fair employment practices, provide enough time between programs to adequately recover, and maintain a positive, inclusive work culture. We take our overriding duty to protecting physical and mental health as seriously for our staff as our participants.
 

Environmental and local impact

We are constantly working to understand, plan, and manage our use of resources to be fair, efficient, and practical. On our programs we support the communities we work with by purposefully using local infrastructure and business.
 




Our Team

Highly qualified and ready to lead, our team of program coordinators, support staff, and group leaders work together to deliver an experience you can be delighted with.

 

A wealth of experience

All our staff come to us with extensive experience, appropriate qualifications, and understand how your young people learn. Before working on a trip, all staff become well versed in utilising our internal risk systems, and fulfilment of our educational outcomes.
 

At the top of their game

We take good care of our staff, so they can take good care of you. Your leaders will arrive fresh, well equipped, and carefully selected to meet your teaching aims. Most importantly, they come backed by a support network they know they can use and rely on.
 

Prepared for the future

Our company culture is one of seeking new, better ways of doing things, and we are always searching to bring home the very best people, tools, and information. We are always learning, and growing.
