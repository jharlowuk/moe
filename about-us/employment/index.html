Field Vacancies

Group Leaders

 

Job Description

Group Leaders work directly in the field leading groups of young people on outdoor education, service, and overlanding programs. In this role, you will be responsible for leading basic activities, group management, program logistics, and delivering on our clients educational expectations. Whilst on program, you will need to maintain a high level of energy, remain in line with our risk standards, and competently respond to incidents according to MOE protocols. We offer work on a seasonal basis, although additional work may be offered year round.

Staff Development blocks starting year round. Based in Sheffield.
 

Required

- 21+ years old
- 3+ years experience working directly with young people in a similar capacity
- 2+ NGB's (SML, WML, MIA, RCI, BCU, LCMLA, Lifeguarding) or equivalent
- Wilderness First Responder (60+ hrs) or equivalent
- Possess a clean driving record
- Successfully pass criminal background and national sex offender checks
- Ability to sustain high-level of activity for 25 days
- Commitment to the full duration of rostered MOE programs
 

Desirable

- Applicable NGB's (SML, WML, MIA, RCI, BCU, LCMLA, Lifeguarding)
- Qualifications and experience in group facilitation and teaching
- Extensive and well rounded outdoor, travel, and volunteer experience.
 

Compensation

- Competitive daily rates of pay, based on experience and qualifications
- Flexible, fair workplace invested in your professional development
- Access to professional discounts on outdoor products
 

How to Apply

Applications will include:
- Cover Letter and Resume
- Logbook of experience
- Applicable First Aid and NGB certificates
- Two current contactable professional references

Please submit these details, or direct questions regarding this posting to employment@monomyth
