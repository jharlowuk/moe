Once you complete a trip with us, you become

 

Trails Alumni

 

Here are three benefits:

1

Enrolment on Voyageur and Serendipity trips

 

Now we know you a little better, it's easier to place you on our harder trails.

2

An online community space

 

Keep in touch with other alumni to organise your own trips, without adverts from us.

3

Discounts on future trips

 

Every Trails Alumni receives a 10% discount on future trips, and a generous refer-a-friend discount.

And don't forget your Trails Alumni patch.

Sew it onto a jacket or pack, and wear with pride!
