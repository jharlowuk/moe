We are

MONOMYTH OUTDOOR EDUCATION


We run high quality, adventurous outdoor education programs for young people.

This is our mission statement:

Prepare young people for the real world, by experiencing it.

We believe that nothing prepares young people for life outside of education, than education outside. Our adventures go far and wide, but they always bring home a little more understanding of where they are, and where they're going.

Teach them real world skills along the way.

On a MOE program, young people learn universal skills which will serve them well for life. From making new friends, to beating an uphill battle, we believe that adventure is where fun leaves the comfort zone.

Provide tailored, scalable, and safe experiences.

Our people can create programs to suit your organisations size, structure, and culture. People come to us for a safe pair of hands, which deliver on an unforgettable adventure.

We believe that young people need adventure, now more than ever. To be encouraged to explore, question, discover, and try things for themselves.

 

Because nothing prepares young people for real life, like the real world.
