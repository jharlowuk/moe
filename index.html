TRAILS OUTDOOR GROUP


We provide exceptional outdoor education programs to schools, camps, and private groups that make better people.

This is our mission statement:

Prepare young people for the real world, by experiencing it.

We believe that nothing prepares young people better for life outside of education, than education outside. Our adventures go far and wide, but they always bring home a little more understanding of where they are, and where they're going.

Learn more

Help schools and camps to succeed, by helping their
young people grow.

From industry leading preset courses, to fully tailored curriculum integrated programs, we partner closely with organisations to bring education outdoors. On every Trails program, young people learn universal skills which will serve them well for life, and help them beat future uphill battles.

Learn more

Provide tailored, scalable, and safe experiences.

Our people can create programs to suit your organisations size, structure, and culture. People come to us for a safe pair of hands, which deliver on an unforgettable adventure.

Learn more

We believe that young people need adventure, now more than ever. To be encouraged to explore, question, discover, and try things for themselves.

 

Because nothing prepares young people for real life, like the real world.
